About Us

Sai Krishna Foundation is a charitable organisation based in the Serlingampalli constituency of Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation. The foundation believes that one of the finer ways of helping the needy is by giving them a hand up rather than a handout. Driven by this ‘civic sense of duty’, the charity focuses on providing support to children and families through awareness programs in the society aiming to empower men, women and the youth as a humanitarian response to the poor.
